How Fitment Factor Works

The fitment factor is a multiplier used to convert your current basic pay to the new pay scale. For example:

If your current basic pay is ₹35,000:
7th CPC (2.57x) → ₹89,950
8th CPC (3.0x projected) → ₹1,05,000 (+₹15,050)
